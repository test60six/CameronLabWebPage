  • Block Retrieval - per case, $30.00 ($10/case if more than one case is ordered)
  • Pathology Consultaion - Review, per hour $250.00
  • RNA Assessment Cut sections (8-10 µm) - per section $15.00 2 - 4 sections depending on size of tissue, includes transport of sections to Cameron Lab in BRB
  • Pre-testing Histology Quality Assurance - FFPE Tissue Block QC Per Slide $350.00 (score blocks, cut sections(5 µm ), dry overnight in dessicator, bake 2 hrs at 42° C, manual deparaffinization, dehydrate, bake, alcohol washes, water wash, manual H&E stain and coverslipping, pathologist inspection to look for tissue detachment (~8 hrs tech time).
  • Visium Slide -Prepare Visium Test Slide, Per slide $350.00.   • Digital Imaging - DP200 slide digitization. Per slide $25.00. After imaging, slide transported back to Cameron Lab
