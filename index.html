The applied Functional Genomics Core is a full service core offering excelence in RNA/DNA extraction, purification, and quality analysis. Our services include single cell RNA sequencing, bulk RNA sequencing, and librarary preparation. We specialize in those hard to process low input samples and can produce quality result from minimal starting material. We also offer full service bioinformatics analysis.
